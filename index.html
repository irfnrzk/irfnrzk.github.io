Delivering IoT Awareness in Malaysia

[CREATE], the first of it's kind in Malaysia is a 3-month series of IoT Awareness Program co-organized by REKA and TM RE with the mission to educate, improve and deepened the knowledge of innovators, makers, students or engineers about IoT.

Partners & Collaborators


This would be the first of this kind of movement in Malaysia and will be a kickstart to those whom have yet to grasp the fundamentals of IoT. We have prepared a few phases towards building a community that is fully aware of IoT and the potential of what they can create in the future. We will start by creating awareness to ensure more innovators are on board with IoT and the impacts of Industry 4.0 which will be held from September to November. There will be a series of workshops and talks designed to capture the interest and cultivate a community of IoT-driven innovators held at participating partners’ venue.

ACTIVITIES

Event Launching

A half-day event that will be held on the 23rd of September at TM Convention Center.

  • ● Opening speech by REKA and TM

  • ● 4 speakers

  •   (a) Business (impact and future of IoT in business)

  •   (b) Industry (impact and future of IoT in industry)

  •   (c) Education (impact and importance in IoT for education)

  •   (d) Testimonial (why choose IoT)

  • ● Closing speech

  • ● Networking session for all partners and invited speakers

IoT & Tech Talk

A 1-2 hours talk held few times a month durin the series at participating partners' venue.

  • ● Opening spech partners

  • ● Talk on IoT and Tech by REKA

  • ● Q&A session

IoT Workshop

A full day workshop held a few times a month during the series at participating partners’ venue.

  • ● Opening speech Partners

  • ● Kick-off with theoretical knowledge of IoT

  • ● A hands-on session of IoT

  • ● Q&A session

Hackathon & Closing

The [CREATE] IoT Series will end with a 24 hours hackathon focusing on building IoTs.

  • ● Participation from public and participants of IoT Talks & Workshops

  • ● Free entry for IoT Workshop Participants

  • ● Details are being finalized
